# Privacy Policy - Moonlight

**Last updated: March 24, 2026**

## What we collect

- **Birth chart info** (sun sign, rising sign, moon sign, birth time): Stored locally on your device only. Never sent to our servers.
- **Location**: Used to calculate accurate moon phase data for your area. Not stored or shared.
- **Questions you ask**: Sent to our AI service to generate readings. Not stored after the reading is delivered.

## What we don't collect

- We don't collect your name, email, or phone number.
- We don't track your activity across other apps.
- We don't sell any data to third parties.
- We don't use analytics or advertising SDKs.

## Third-party services

- **Anthropic (Claude AI)**: Your questions are processed by Claude AI to generate readings. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to this processing.
- **FreeAstrologyAPI**: Your approximate location and current time are used to calculate planetary positions. No personal data is shared.
- **Apple StoreKit**: In-app purchases are handled by Apple. Apple's privacy policy applies.

## Data storage

All personal preferences (birth chart info, credit balance) are stored locally on your device using iOS built-in storage. We have no server-side database of user data.

## Children

Moonlight is not intended for children under 13.
